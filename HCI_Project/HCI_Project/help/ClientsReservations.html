Reserved tickets

On this page you can see all of the tickets you have reserved.
You have options to confirm or cancel a reservation. By confirming a reservation, you purchase the ticket.
If you do not confirm a reservation at least 3 days before departure, it will be automatically cancelled.
To select a reserved ticket and to see departure details, click on the table row that represents it.

You can also filter your tickets by date of reservation.
To choose the lower boundary, click on the calendar icon on the right side of Pick start date field and choose a date from the calendar.
To choose the upper boundary, click on the calendar icon on the right side of Pick end date field and choose a date from the calendar.
Another way is to enter wanted date directly in Pick end date or Pick start date field in dd/MM/yyyy format.

To display filtered tickets, click on the button Filter.

To remove a filter, click on the X button or the right of the chosen date.
Then click on the button Filter to refresh results.

To confirm a selected reservation, click on the Confirm button.
After that, you will be able to see the ticket with the rest of your purchased tickets.

To cancel a selected reservation, click on the Cancel button.
