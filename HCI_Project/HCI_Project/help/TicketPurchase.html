Search for departures and purchase a ticket

On this page you can search for departures and select one for which you want to purchase or reserve a ticket.
In order to search for departures, you must first enter your starting destination, final destination and date of departure.

Start typing in Starting destination and Final destination fields to search for your starting and final destination.
You can click on one of the suggested options to choose it.

To enter date of departure, you can click on the calendar icon on the right side of Date of departure field and choose a date from calendar.
Another way is to enter wanted date directly in Date of departure field in dd/MM/yyyy format. Note thay you cannot choose past dates.

Click on the Show button to display departures going from your start destination to final destination on chosen date.

To see details of departure and to select it, click on the table row that represents wanted departure.

Click on button Choose a seat to choose a seat and wagon on the train for selected departure
