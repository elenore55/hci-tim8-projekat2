Welcome!

This application is here to help you explore train departures for which you can purchase or reserve tickets

To manage your tickets, click on the Tickets menu item and choose one of the options.
To reserve or purchase a ticket click on the Reserve or purchase option.
To see the tickets you have purchased, click on the My tickets option.
To see and manage the tickets you have reserved, click on the My reservations option.

To explore existing train lines, click on the Lines menu item and choose the Network option.
