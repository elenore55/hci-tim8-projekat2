Train edit

On this page you can add new train by adding wagons, their dimensions and train name.

Step 1:

Click on the + Add wagon button on the left side of the page. Then, you will see new canvas in which you should enter wagon information.

Step 2:

Enter the number of rows in enw wagon.

Step 3:

Enter the number of seats in each row in new wagon.

Step 4:

Click on the arrow in the corner of the field and choose wagon class.

Step 5:

If you want to apply changes, click on confirm button.

Step 6:

You must also enter new train's name before saving train.

Step 7:

When you have finished entering the information, click Save train button on the bottom right corner of the page. If all fields are properly filled, you will receive message about your operation's succes. If there are errors, you will also receive message about it.
