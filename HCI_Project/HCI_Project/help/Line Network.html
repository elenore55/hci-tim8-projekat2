Preview lines network and their information

On this page you can choose and preview line network, see route, stations and departures!

This is first screen you see!

1 - Here you can choose line

Click on one of the listed lines to choose

When line is choosen, the line info will load. This is the example!

1 - This is map where you can see route and it's stops! You can zoom in on the map to see exact stop location.

2 - This is stations list, all stations of the line are listed here. Here you see name and travel time of the station from the first station!

3 - This is departures list, all departures of the line are listed here. Here you see departure time and arival time of departure!
