Purchased tickets

On this page you can see all of the tickets you have purchased
To see departure details of a certain ticket, click on the table row that represents it.

You can also filter your tickets by date of purchase.
To choose the lower boundary, click on the calendar icon on the right side of Pick start date field and choose a date from the calendar.
To choose the upper boundary, click on the calendar icon on the right side of Pick end date field and choose a date from the calendar.
Another way is to enter wanted date directly in Pick end date or Pick start date field in dd/MM/yyyy format.

To display filtered tickets, click on the button Filter.

To remove a filter, click on the X button or the right of the chosen date.
Then click on the button Filter to refresh results.
